Part 1: Ray Generation and Intersection

The effects that causes the difference of m = 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 100 is explained as follow.

For m = 1, the ray successfully hits on the ball with reflection, but refraction requires 2 bounces.
Now m = 2, the refraction succeeded, but the reflection ray needs another iteration.
